Welcome to BunnyHaven

Give Love & Hope to
Every Bunny

BunnyHaven is a non-profit rescue organization dedicated to saving, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned and neglected rabbits. Together, we can make every hop count.

Safe Shelter

Every rescued bunny is provided with a clean, comfortable, and loving temporary home.

Healthy Meals

We provide fresh veggies, hay, and water daily to ensure every bunny thrives.

Loving Care

Our volunteers offer socialization, cuddles, and medical care for every rabbit.

Adoption Programs

We connect kind-hearted families with their future fluffy companions.

Join us — help every hop find a home.

Our Story

Together, We Build A Safe Haven for Bunnies

Our Mission

Founded in 2024, BunnyHaven has rescued and rehomed over 200 rabbits. We believe every bunny deserves a chance at a happy, healthy life filled with love and safety.

Our Vision

Expand our rescue network, increase foster participation, and provide education so all bunnies find loving homes.

Next Plan

  • Bunny rescue and rehabilitation
  • Community education
  • Volunteer-driven programs
  • Affordable adoption

Bunny Care & Rescue Services

Comprehensive services designed to keep every rabbit healthy, safe and happy.

Rescue and Shelter

Rapid response rescue for stranded or surrendered rabbits and safe short-term sheltering.

Medical Care and Rehab

On-site basic medical triage and coordination with vets for rehabilitation and recovery.

Nutrition and Feeding

Balanced diets, hay, fresh vegetables and feeding plans tailored to each bunny's needs.

Adoption Programs

Thorough screening and matching to ensure long-term, loving placements for our rabbits.
